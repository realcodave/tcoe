THE CHURCH OF EVANGELISM

HISTORY

Reverend Dr. Edith Adefunke Okubanjo is the founder of The Church Of Evangelism

She was born in Lagos on July 22 1953 into the family of Reverend Adegbite Daniel Tella who pastored the Ebenezer Baptist Church, located on Campbel St, Lagos for 37 years, before he went to be with the Lord in 1964. Until his untimely passing in 2012, she was married to Elder Oloye Oladotun Okunowo Okubanjo in England, one of the greatest supporters of the Church and her ministry.

Rev Edith Adefunke Okubanjo received the call of God upon her life in 1982 while she was studying abroad in the United States of America.

On that fateful night, our Lord Jesus Christ appeared to her as she slept, anointed her with oil, and instructed her to go and preach the gospel; to heal the sick, and set those who have been held spiritually captive free. She accepted her charge, and has been doing so ever since.

The name of The Church of Evangelism was revealed to her by the Almighty God during a night prayer section in 2001. She rose and answered the call, founding the Church of Evangelism at its home base inOsapa, Lekki

Through her unceasing ministrations and efforts many sick have been healed in the name of Jesus Christ of Nazareth.

PURPOSE OF THE CHURCH

Many people grow up attending church, yet if you ask them to biblically define the purpose of the church, not many can. The understanding of church goes back to the time of Christ Jesus on earth. During that time, most people spoke Greek and it is from this language we get the English word for church which is "Ekklesia". To the ancient Greeks, this meant "an assembly", or "a gathering". The Church of Evangelism (TCE) is an assembly, a gathering of God's people.

Scripture is clear about how we, as Christians, should behave and operate in the church: with the power of love, unity and teamwork. TCE seeks to show others the same type of love that our Lord Jesus has shown us.

"By this shall all men know that ye are my disciples, if ye have love one another." (John 11:35).
To work effectively, we need to exhibit the love that Jesus spoke about.

People are attracted by love. When they see workers who flow together in genuine love, they are attracted. To foster love, unity,teamwork among church workers, the Church of Evangelism has put togetherthismanual with the help of the Holy Spirit. It is our prayer thatthis manual will cause TCE workers to be effective in their service for God and that such effectiveness will give rise to the manifestaion of unconditional love (modeled by Our Lord, Jesus Christ) that will permeate every aspect of the church.
